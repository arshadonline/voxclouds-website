FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a setup fee?

No setup fees on any plan. Your first month is prorated so you only pay for days used.

Can I keep my existing number?

Yes. We support number porting from most carriers. The process takes 3-10 business days.

What's the minimum commitment?

Month-to-month on all plans. No contracts, cancel anytime. Annual plans get 2 months free.

Do wholesale rates include all destinations?

Yes. Our A-Z rate deck covers 150+ countries. Special pricing is available for high-volume Africa and Middle East routes.

Can I use VoxClouds with my existing PBX?

Absolutely. Our SIP trunks work with Asterisk, FreeSWITCH, 3CX, Cisco, and any SIP-compliant system.

How do I connect an AI agent?

We provide SIP credentials that work with Retell AI, VAPI, Bland.ai, ElevenLabs and Synthflow. Full documentation available after signup.
